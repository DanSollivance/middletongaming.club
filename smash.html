SUPER SMASH BROS.

ABOUT

Super Smash Bros is a series of crossover fighting games published by Nintendo, that primarily featuring characters from franchises established on its systems. The gameplay objective differs from that of traditional fighters by aiming to knock opponents out of the stage instead of depleting life bars. The series features many characters from Nintendo's most popular franchises, including Mario, Fox, Link, Kirby, Samus and Pikachu. Every title in the series has been well received by critics, with much praise given to the multiplayer mode experience. The Super Smash Bros. games have spawned a large competitive community and have been featured in several high profile gaming tournaments. In our club we are going to be teaching and competing in Super Smash Bros. 4 for the Wii U and 3DS, and we are planning on a transition into Smash Ultimate when it releases later this year.
