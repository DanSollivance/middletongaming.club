LEAGUE OF LEGENDS

ABOUT

League of Legends is a competitive MOBA game developed by Riot Games in which 10 players on 2 teams of 5 fight head to head. Teams battle it out on Summoner’s Rift where the objective is to push and fight your way into your opponent’s base to destroy their nexus before they destroy yours. Each player controls a champion, each with different abilities, making each match unique and exciting. There are many objectives on the map as well as towers that you must fight through to reach your opponent’s nexus. Each year teams from all over the world gather to compete for the Summoner’s cup and a chance to bring fame to their nation as well as claim the title of top League of Legends team in the world.
