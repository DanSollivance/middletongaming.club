HEROES OF THE STORM

ABOUT

Heroes of the Storm is a MOBA developed by Blizzard which pits 10 players on 2 teams of 5 in a head to head battle. Teams fight on several different maps, each with different objectives and goals. Complete these goals to help your team push into your opponent’s base and destroy their core. Teams must fight over objectives to ensure that their opponent does not destroy their core first. Each player on the team controls a Hero, and each hero has different abilities which changes how each and every game plays out, making each match unique.
