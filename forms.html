Middleton High School E-Sports Club

We are a club driven by the idea of competitive video gaming as a sport and as an outlet of skill, strategy, and teamwork. The industry of eSports grows exponentially year by year, and we aim to prepare our club members to dive into the world of competitive gaming, while also building real-world skills in sportsmanship, cooperation, and a healthy sense of competition.
