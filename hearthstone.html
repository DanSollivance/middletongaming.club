HEARTHSTONE

ABOUT

Hearthstone is a strategic collectible card game taking place within the Warcraft Universe by Blizzard Entertainment. Summon creatures, cast spells, and use the powers of your classes to defeat your opponent. This is a turn-based strategy game with a resouce system (mana) that steadily increases with each turn, allowing eacher player to play more powerful cards. A variety of different deck styles and archetypes can be made using combinations of hundreds of different cards, promoting diverse gameplay and depth.
